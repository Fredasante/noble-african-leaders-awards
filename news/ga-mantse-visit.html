Courtesy Call on His Royal Majesty, the Ga Mantse

15th August 2025   |   Event Highlight

The Noble African Leaders Awards (NALA) team had the honour of paying a courtesy call on His Royal Majesty, the Ga Mantse, as part of our ongoing preparations towards the maiden edition of the awards ceremony. This visit formed an important step in strengthening relationships with traditional leadership and aligning the awards with cultural values that embody the true spirit of Africa.

During the meeting, our delegation formally shared the vision and mission of the Noble African Leaders Awards — an initiative dedicated to recognizing and celebrating exceptional African leaders who exemplify excellence, service, and unity. We highlighted the importance of showcasing leaders whose impact transcends borders, uplifts communities, and contributes to Africa’s transformation on the global stage.

As a gesture of goodwill, the NALA team made a symbolic donation to His Majesty, demonstrating our values of generosity, community engagement, and partnership. This act also underscored our belief that leadership is not only about recognition but also about service and contribution.

The moment was beautifully marked with photographs alongside His Majesty. Our team members, proudly wearing our branded NALA T-shirts, embodied the identity of a movement committed to Uniting Africa, Honouring Excellence & Leadership. The images capture the spirit of collaboration and hope that guides our journey.

This encounter with the Ga Mantse is more than a ceremonial courtesy; it represents a cultural and symbolic alignment with Africa’s heritage, values, and traditions. The Ga State, known for its rich history and influence, provides a significant backdrop to an initiative that seeks to unite diverse leadership voices from across the continent.

As we move closer to the grand event, we are encouraged by the positive reception and the support we continue to receive from leaders, institutions, and communities. The Noble African Leaders Awards is not just an event, but a platform to inspire a new generation of leaders who are bold, ethical, and visionary.

We look forward to sharing more milestones with you in the coming weeks as preparations intensify. The countdown has begun towards a memorable evening of honouring Africa’s finest, celebrating resilience, and highlighting the leaders shaping the future of our continent.
